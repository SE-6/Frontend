Welcome to Dragon's Den

Gather your party, roll the dice, and enjoy handcrafted coffee in a cozy tavern-like setting. We host weekly D&D campaigns, board game nights, and beginner-friendly sessions.

About Us

Dragon's Den started as a small group of friends who wanted a friendly space to play tabletop games and socialize. We offer a curated library of board games, D&D modules, experienced Dungeon Masters, and a menu of specialty coffees and snacks.

Our Mission

Make tabletop gaming welcoming, accessible, and fun for everyone — from first-time players to seasoned adventurers.

Events & Services

  • Weekly D&D campaigns (beginner & advanced tables)
  • Board game nights and tournaments
  • Private bookings for parties and corporate team-building
  • Snack and beverage menu with themed specials
